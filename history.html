Who created Mario?

1985

Super Mario was created by Shigeru Miyamoto.
Initially known as Jumpman, later, Nintendo President Minoru Arawaka renamed him after the owner of Nintendo's first warehouse.
In 1985, he made his debut in the first game entirely dedicated to him, titled "Super Mario Bros.".

1996

With tremendous success, Mario ventured into the 3D gaming realm in 1996 with his first 3D game, "Super Mario 64".
This game set a new standard for 3D platformers and will be remembered for its revolution in the world of video games.

2017

Super Mario Odyssey, released in 2017 for the Nintendo Switch, continued Mario's legacy.
In this game, Mario explores various kingdoms, using a magical hat named Cappy to possess objects and enemies.
The game received widespread acclaim for its innovative gameplay and design, adding another successful chapter to the iconic Super Mario series.
