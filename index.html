Who is Mario?

Super Mario is the iconic character from Nintendo, known for his heroic adventures, rescuing Princess Peach from the villainous Bowser, and navigating various challenging levels filled with enemies and obstacles.
Mario, with his red hat, mustache, and blue overalls, lives in the Mushroom Kingdom and works as a plumber.
